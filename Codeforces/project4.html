This warm, comforting noodle dish has taken the world by storm and, rightly so. Its apparent simplicity belies complex flavours that are at once unctuous and refreshing. Born in northern Vietnam in the late 19th century, pho’s development was influenced by Chinese and French cooking, mirroring the history of the country. Today it is a uniquely Vietnamese offering that you won’t have to go far to find wherever you are in the world.
